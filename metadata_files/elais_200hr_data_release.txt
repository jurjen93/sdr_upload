In this data release, we present ultra-deep sub-arcsecond resolution images and source catalogues at 115–168 MHz
covering a 2.5×2.5 square degree field of view centred on the ELAIS-N1 deep field.
The data has been produced using 200 hours of LOFAR-HBA observations, including all international stations, obtained from 25 epochs.
The data were processed using the automated direction-dependent calibration pipelines LINC, the DDF-pipeline, and PILOT, followed by imaging with WSClean to produce primary beam-corrected Stokes I images.
The resulting mosaics have resolutions of 0.36″×0.45″, 0.58″×0.62″, and 1.0″×1.5″, with corresponding best sensitivities of 5.6 μJy beam⁻¹, 8.0 μJy beam⁻¹, and 14.1 μJy beam⁻¹, respectively.
